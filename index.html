Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

The game saves automatically. You can also save manually in Options, with up to 3 save slots available.

Can I change the game language?

Yes! Go to Options in the game and choose from Portuguese, English, Spanish, Italian, German, and Japanese.

I lost my data, what should I do?

Data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstalled the app, unfortunately the data cannot be recovered. Please contact us for further assistance.

The app is crashing, what should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, update to the latest version from the App Store or contact us via the email above.

About WorldFoot

WorldFoot is a football management game where you take charge of a Brazilian Serie A club. Manage your squad, negotiate transfers, set tactics, and lead your team to glory!
